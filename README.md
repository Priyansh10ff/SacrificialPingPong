# Sacrificial Ping Pong

A unique retro arcade twist on the classic pong game. In this version, every time the ball hits your paddle, a piece of your paddle is destroyed, leaving a gap. 

## Features

- **Destructible Paddles**: Every hit destroys a segment of your paddle.
- **Dynamic AI**: The computer opponent tracks the ball and becomes more accurate as you score points.
- **Local Multiplayer**: Play against a friend on the same device.
- **Power-ups**:
  - **Green (E)**: Expands your paddle to fill the screen width temporarily.
  - **Red (S)**: Shrinks your opponent's paddle.
  - **Yellow (F)**: Gives the ball a speed boost.
- **Settings**: Adjust ball speed, winning score, and AI difficulty.

## Setup

1. Download the three files: `index.html`, `style.css`, and `script.js`.
2. Place them all in the same folder.
3. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.

## Controls

### Keyboard

- **Player 1 (Bottom)**: `Arrow Left` / `Arrow Right` OR `A` / `D`
- **Player 2 (Top)**: `A` / `D`
- **Pause**: `P` key

### Gamepad

- **Move**: Left Analog Stick or D-Pad
- **Pause**: Start Button

### Touch

- Touch and drag on your half of the screen to move your paddle.
